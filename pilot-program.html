WHY WE CREATED THE PILOT

Great Dealerships Don't Need Another Software Project

We understand the challenges dealerships face when evaluating new technology.

  • Concerns around adoption
  • Concerns around CRM integration
  • Concerns around customer data
  • Concerns around disruption

The Founding Dealership Pilot Program was created to remove those barriers.

Rather than asking dealerships to change how they operate, TrustDriver is designed to operate alongside existing systems and processes.

ZERO OPERATIONAL RISK

Your CRM Stays.
Your Data Stays.
Your Process Continues.

TrustDriver was specifically designed to complement existing dealership systems.

  • Customer information can be synchronised back into your CRM.
  • Existing workflows continue.
  • Customer communications can be aligned with existing dealership processes.

If you choose not to continue after the pilot, your customer information remains available inside your existing environment.
